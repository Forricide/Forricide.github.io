Coil activated his power.

He found himself surrounded yet again by several of his own men - Britters, for shame! - and the Undersiders, helmed by that girl. She was wearing a different costume as always. This time, her mask had several strangely placed holes: one in the middle of her forehead, revealing nothing but shadow; one on her left cheek, an eye bizarrely staring out; one where her mouth should have been, but there was only skin.

She was speaking, and he realized her mouth was visible through the top hole, only barely.

"No, I think-"

Coil activated his power.

He found himself in the same street, in the same position. Britters was still there, among assorted mercenaries Coil could barely remember the names of. Tattletale, Grue, and the rest of the Undersiders stood in front of him.

This time, she was wearing a flashier costume, cartoonish lightning bolts striking down all sides of a sky-blue suit.

She was finishing a sentence, and although he had the impression she wasn't putting much effort into speaking, the words boomed all the same: "-it does."

Coil activated his power.

She was there, the Undersiders still backing her up, although Grue looked a bit worse for wear. She was above him, and it took him a second to realize she was floating, standing on a bizarre contraption that appeared to be old keyboards with their PS/2 cables plugged into an elderly woman who was hovering several metres above the ground. He wasn't sure what was more concerning: that the woman seemed to still be alive, or that - as far as he could tell - she was enjoying herself.

The girl was holding a gun, and was looking straight at him.

Coil activated his power.
